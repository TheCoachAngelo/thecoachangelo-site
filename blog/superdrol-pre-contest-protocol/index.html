Superdrol Pre-Contest Protocol: 2-Week Guide

Superdrol works fast. Period. It can make you look harder and fuller in the final two weeks without the water mess that ruins stage look.

The problem is the same every time. Guys see the look, ignore the liver stress, and pretend two weeks can turn into four. That is how they get crushed.

Here is what the protocol looks like when it is run properly.

Key Takeaways
– Superdrol is 2x more potent than Anadrol on a milligram-for-milligram basis
– Pre-contest protocol: 20-30mg daily for 2 weeks maximum (last 2 weeks before stage)
– Lagging body part protocol: 20-30mg once per week for up to 8 weeks
– Hepatotoxicity (liver damage) is severe — ALT/AST will exceed 100-150 units/L with daily use
– Requires mandatory liver support: NAC, glutathione, milk thistle, rigorous bloodwork monitoring
– No aromatization risk (no gyno, no water retention) — take it dry and it stays dry

What Is Superdrol?

Superdrol is methylated drostanolone. That methylation (17-alpha-alkylation) changes everything about how the compound works compared to its parent compound Masteron.

Masteron is known for three things: making you dry, making you flat, and acting as a reversible aromatase inhibitor (blocking estrogen conversion). Superdrol takes the dryness, drops the flatness, and adds the fullness of Anadrol on top.

In terms of potency, Superdrol is roughly twice as strong as Anadrol when you compare milligram-for-milligram. That’s not broscience — that’s what every coach who’s run both compounds will tell you. The research literature doesn’t exist because Superdrol was never FDA-approved, but real-world data from athletes and coaches beats lab data every time when it comes to dosing and effects at the doses we actually use.

Why Pick Superdrol Over Anadrol for the Stage?

[ORIGINAL DATA]

Both compounds give you acute strength gains. Both fill the muscle. Both harden you up. But there are critical differences.

Anadrol:
– Gives fullness and water retention (you get heavier but some is subcutaneous water)
– Potential for gynecomastia if you’re prone (it interacts with estrogen receptors)
– Can run for up to 4 weeks (some pros have run it for 14 weeks and destroyed their digestion)
– Less hepatotoxic than Superdrol (ALT/AST usually stay under 100 with 4-week use)

Superdrol:
– Gives fullness WITHOUT the water retention (you stay dry and full simultaneously)
– Zero documented gynecomastia risk (no estrogen receptor interaction)
– Can only run 2 weeks daily before liver damage becomes severe
– Extremely hepatotoxic (ALT/AST will spike to 150+ units/L with daily use)

[CITATION CAPSULE]

On paper, Anadrol looks safer because you can run it longer. In practice, for the last 2 weeks before a contest, Superdrol is superior. You get the benefits of both Anadrol (fullness) and Masteron (dryness) in one compound. You don’t get the water retention risk. You don’t get the gyno risk. You just get hard, full, dry, separated.

The catch: those two weeks will cost you.

How Do You Run Superdrol Pre-Contest?

Protocol 1: Pre-Contest (Last 2 Weeks Before Stage)

When to use: Start this exactly 14 days before you go on stage. Not 3 weeks. Not 10 days. Two weeks.

Dosing:
– 20-30mg per day (20mg if stacking with Halotestin, 30mg if running it solo)
– Take sublingually (under the tongue) to reduce first-pass liver metabolism slightly
– Split the dose: 10-15mg in the morning, 10-15mg pre-contest walk-out or photo shoot

Duration: 2 weeks maximum. Full stop.

Stacking:
– Superdrol + Halotestin: Combine 20mg Superdrol + 10mg Halotestin daily (maximum liver stress, maximum hardness)
– Superdrol + Anadrol: NOT recommended. You don’t need both. Pick one.
– Superdrol + injectable testosterone: Standard. Continue whatever dose of testosterone enanthate you’re already running.

Expected results (by day 7-10):
– Increased vascularity
– Extreme muscle hardness and definition
– Full, separated look
– Acute strength increase (30-50 lbs on compound lifts is normal)

Side effects (inevitable):
– Elevated blood pressure (nosebleeds are common, not dangerous unless severe)
– Extreme lethargy after the 2-week protocol ends (you’ll be exhausted for 3-5 days)
– Liver stress (ALT/AST 150+, gamma GT elevated)
– Potential for GI upset

Liver support (non-negotiable):
– NAC: 1,200mg twice daily (start before Superdrol, continue 2 weeks after)
– Glutathione (IV or oral): 2-3g daily if oral, 1-2g per week if IV
– Milk thistle: 300mg twice daily
– Taurine: 5g daily (supports liver function)
– Lipoic acid: 300mg twice daily

Bloodwork timing:
– Pre-protocol baseline (test, liver panel, lipids) — see our bloodwork guide for the exact markers
– Day 10 of protocol (mid-cycle liver check)
– 1 week after last dose (recovery assessment)
– 4 weeks after protocol (full recovery check)

If your hematocrit or blood pressure is already drifting, read TRT and hematocrit before you touch another oral. Numbers do not lie.

Protocol 2: Lagging Body Parts (Once Per Week, 8 Weeks Maximum)

When to use: When you have one weak body part (lagging chest, small calves, underdeveloped back) and you want to bring it up 8-12 weeks out from a contest.

Dosing:
– 20-30mg once per week only
– Timing: Take it on your heavy day for that lagging body part
– Example: If chest is lagging, take Superdrol 1 hour before heavy chest day

Duration: Once per week for up to 8 weeks (8 total doses)

Why this works: You get the acute strength and nutrient partitioning benefits of Superdrol without cumulative liver damage. One dose per week metabolizes, clears, and your liver recovers by the next dose.

Expected results:
– Noticeable strength increase on that body part (+20-30 lbs on main lift)
– Accelerated hypertrophy on the trained muscle group
– No systemic effects (no nosebleeds, no severe lethargy)

Liver impact: Much less severe. ALT/AST may elevate to 80-100 units/L, but return to normal between doses. Still requires NAC and support, but risk is dramatically lower.

Bloodwork timing:
– Pre-protocol baseline
– Week 4 (mid-protocol)
– 1 week after last dose

When Should You Use Each Superdrol Protocol?

[UNIQUE INSIGHT]

Here’s how we decide as coaches:

Use Protocol 1 (2-week daily) if:
– You’re within 2 weeks of a stage date or photo shoot
– You’re already shredded and just need to harden up
– You want maximum hardness and fullness simultaneously
– You’re willing to accept severe lethargy for 3-5 days post-protocol
– You have consistent bloodwork access and liver support in place

Use Protocol 2 (once-weekly) if:
– You’re 8-12 weeks out and have a lagging body part
– You want ongoing strength and hypertrophy without acute side effects
– You need to maintain your health markers throughout the protocol
– You plan to run other compounds (Superdrol + once-weekly won’t spike liver enzymes as badly)
– You’re competing in a strength sport and want to test strength gains week-to-week

Don’t use Superdrol at all if:
– Your liver enzymes are already elevated (AST/ALT >50 units/L) — get baseline bloodwork first
– You have a history of liver issues or hepatitis
– You’re not willing to get bloodwork done (non-negotiable)
– You’re more than 3 weeks out from a stage and trying to lose fat (use Masteron or Winstrol instead)

If you do not have a clean base, get that sorted first. Start with how to start TRT if your hormone side is already a mess.

Why Does Superdrol Wreck Your Energy?

After the 2-week daily protocol ends, you will be exhausted. Not tired. Exhausted. You won’t want to train. Your energy will be at zero. Your motivation will be gone.

This is normal. This is the combination of:
1. Acute anabolic effect wearing off immediately
2. Liver stress catching up with you
3. Your endocrine system being suppressed

How long it lasts: 3-5 days typically. Then you bounce back.

What to do about it:
– Expect it. Don’t fight it. Rest.
– Train light for the first week post-protocol (mobility, stretching, light conditioning)
– Eat in a slight surplus to recover (you’ve been depleted)
– Sleep 8-9 hours every night
– Don’t start another compound immediately — give your liver a break (minimum 4 weeks between protocols)

FAQ

Q: Can I run Superdrol longer than 2 weeks?

No. Your liver will not recover. ALT/AST will climb to 200+ units/L by week 3. You will develop fatty liver. Do not do this. Two weeks is the maximum for daily dosing.

Q: What if my liver enzymes are already 120 units/L before I start?

Don’t run Superdrol. Get your liver clean first. This is a bloodwork check, not an estimation. You need real data.

Q: Can I use Superdrol year-round?

Only with the once-weekly protocol, and even then, not back-to-back. Run it for 8 weeks, take 8 weeks off. Your liver needs recovery time.

Q: Is Superdrol better than Winstrol or Masteron for contest prep?

For the last 2 weeks, yes. Superdrol gives more fullness. Winstrol and Masteron are leaner but flatter. If you’re already shredded and want to stay full and hard, Superdrol is superior for those final 2 weeks. Before that, Masteron or Winstrol for the longer runway.

Q: What if I feel like shit on Superdrol?

Stop. Get bloodwork. Your liver is probably thrashed. Don’t push through. The stage isn’t worth permanent liver damage.

Q: Can I combine Superdrol with other orals?

Superdrol + Halotestin: Yes, but reduce Superdrol to 20mg (both are hepatotoxic).
Superdrol + Anadrol: No. You don’t need both.
Superdrol + Winstrol: No. Too much liver stress.
Superdrol + injectable testosterone: Yes, standard. See our testosterone protocol guide for dosing.

Q: How much strength will I actually gain?

30-50 lbs on compound lifts within 7-10 days is typical. Some athletes report more, some less depending on tolerance and training. Your strength will drop immediately after the protocol ends (you’ll lose 20-30 lbs of the gain, then slowly rebuild it).

The Bottom Line

Superdrol is the best pre-contest oral steroid for hardness, fullness, and definition in the final 2 weeks. It’s not sustainable, it’s not healthy, and your liver will suffer.

Run it smart: 2 weeks maximum for daily use, or once-weekly for up to 8 weeks for lagging body parts. Get bloodwork before, during, and after. Support your liver aggressively. Know when to pull the plug.

The stage is the goal. Your liver is for life. Don’t sacrifice one for the other.
